About AutoXpress

AutoXpress is Pakistan's premier online car marketplace where buyers and sellers meet. Our mission is to simplify car buying and selling with ease and transparency.

Founded and developed by Waleed Arain, we strive to provide the best experience for car enthusiasts.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell my car?

Fill out the "Sell Your Car" form with your car details and contact information, then submit. Your car will be listed immediately.

Is there any charge to list my car?

No, listing your car on AutoXpress is completely free!

How do I contact a seller?

Contact details of the seller are displayed with each car listing. You can reach out to them via email.

Can I upload pictures of my car?

Yes! Provide an image URL when submitting your car. The picture will show with your listing.
